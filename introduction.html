Company Introduction

Foshan Nanhai Ziann Medical Apparatus Co., Ltd. located in Foshan City, China, the core production base for universal dental unit in the world.Over a decade of development, Ziann today has the large-scale production facilities, covers a factory area of more than 10,000m2 and set manufacturing, sales and service in one of professional dental comprehensive apparatus manufacturers. Ziann products also have passed through the ISO 13485 quality management system certification and the certification CE0123 certified by German TUV.
